STUDENT COUNCIL 2023-24

The investiture ceremony is a big day for the students when the office bearers are given badges and sashes of office as they pledge their loyalty to the school. Parents are invited to witness the proud moment. For greater vertical intermingling, the school is divided into four houses namely: Chandra, Indra, Surya and Varun. Each child is allotted a house and inter-house co-curriculum activities form an important part of the school calendar.
