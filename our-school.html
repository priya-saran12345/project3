Vission and Mission

Our School

Founded in 1996, by the Gyan Mandir Society in the heart of South Delhi, THE INDIAN SCHOOL prepares students for the Central Board of Secondary Education. The beautiful building with its ample grounds is equipped with naturally well-lit and ventilated classrooms providing an ideal setting for academic rigour and an all – round personality development. We have state-of-the-art labs for Computers, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics & Psychology. Specialized teaching rooms are alloted for Environment and Social Sciences, Entrepreneurship and Business Studies, Fashion Technology, a computerised Teachers’ Resource Centre is also available to keep pace with the multi-learning methods of the future, and to develop the full potential of every child. Our classrooms are equipped with Smartboard technology whereby groups of students can communicate with school children across the world.
We have a department to take care of special needs. The school is inclusive, and child-centred and believes in providing equal opportunities to all children along with those with learning disabilities.

We encourage our children to think independently, to respect themselves and others, and to value excellence.

The Learning Centre with its enlarged library has a Resource Room with audio-visual, internet and photocopying facilities . It also houses the school archives and a Reading Room.

The campus has a football field, a cricket pitch, a state-of-the-art skating rink and a basketball court. It also has an athletic track, spectator stands, an indoor badminton court and tables for table tennis.

We have our Sports, Science and Eco Adventure Park on the outskirts of Delhi. Students plant trees, grow herbs and vegetables and prepare compost. It's a great place for students to visit thoughout the year.

The Theme – of – the – Year Scheme is a part of our innovative pedagogy, where the selected theme runs like a spiral, synergizing the entire school. It has brought joy to learning and enabled the teacher to identify the learning style of each student.
