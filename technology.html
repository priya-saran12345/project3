The Library

Technology Lab

Our School is committed to integrating technology into education.
The content repository consists of thousands of highly animated, lesson-specific, 3D and 2D multimedia modules built with an instructor-led design that allows the teacher to effectively explain the lesson in a typical classroom of a diverse set of learners.

Students have access to classroom audio, video, graphics and 3D animation which makes learning a pleasure.

COMPUTER LABS

We have three state-of-the-art Computer Labs for Junior, Senior and Sr. Secondary wings, spread over an area of nearly 1,000 sq. feet, with 70 fully updated computers.

Broadband and internet connection is available through the network. The Audio Visual room with its resource facilities has a full-sized projection screen to be used for presentation by children, teachers and guest speakers, which aids in making the learning experience multi-dimensional. Computer Science is an integral part of the academic curriculum and is compulsory for students from Class I onwards. NIIT computer aided learning syllabus is integrated with the CBSE curriculum. Students participate in the National Cyber Olympiad conducted by the National Science Foundation, National Informatics and CBSE.

PHYSICS, CHEMISTRY, BIOLOGY, PSYCHOLOGY, ENGINEERING GRAPHICS, FASHION STUDIES AND ENVIRONMENT LABS

We have state-of-the-art Science Labs, one each for Physics, Chemistry and Biology. The labs are equipped with audio-visual (AV) facilities to enable students to stay in touch with the latest developments in science and technology. Our labs have a separate reference section and students have access to the computer.

A SCIENTIFIC BENT OF MIND

The School has developed a Science Activity Centre for primary and middle school. A major Science Exhibition is held by the students each year, to showcase their discoveries and inventions. It is a culmination of the Science Month.

GARNERING ELECTRICITY FROM KINETIC ENERGY

There’s an Open Science Park at our Eco Park on the outskirts of Delhi, an enjoyable excursion for the students at any time of the year.
